Introduction/Explanation:

Have you argued with someone over whether it is smart to peek mid at the start of a round as CT on d2 or T on mirage?
Or whether it is a good idea to swing bottom of mid as a T on inferno?

Usually these discussions go nowhere because both sides only have feelings or at best abstract reasons to support their position.

With this app i want to give you the opportunity to support or refute your argument with some real data.

This app allows you to to determine the CT win percentage for fights filtered by map, victim and attacker position and weapons and fight time. See below for some concrete examples.

However please note that the results shown here are not the be all end all for such discussions.
The filters here are somewhat crude and do not take into account any further context like teammates, nades or bomb status. So you still have to use your brain when interpreting the results.
